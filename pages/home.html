About Adam

Adam Auchie is in Third year of a Bachelor of Music degree at The University of Edinburgh. Originally from Aberdeen, he is the current holder of the James Smart Memorial Organ Scholarship and has achieved recent success at the Edinburgh Competition Festival.

Adam's main passion and experience comes from his piano teaching business that he has been running for five years. He has been providing in-person piano tuition since 2015, and has been providing online tuition since 2016.

When he moved away to University in Edinburgh, around 15 of his Aberdonian students decided to continue lessons online with Adam. Since the beginning of lockdown, Adam successfully doubled his online student base.

In addition to this, Adam works as a technical support agent for a small server hosting company, answering technical and billing enquiries from clients.
